The pie chart highlights UNC Charlotte’s significant achievements in the newly released 2025 U.S. News & World Report Best Graduate Schools rankings. Multiple graduate programs reached their highest-ever placements, underscoring the University’s growing national stature in research and education. Key highlights include the Charlotte MBA, ranked No. 49 nationally and No. 1 among North Carolina’s public universities for part-time programs; the School of Nursing's master’s program, which rose 41 spots to No. 68; and strong showings from programs in Nurse Anesthesia, Special Education, Public Affairs, and Clinical Psychology. These rankings follow UNC Charlotte’s recent Carnegie R1 designation, placing it among the top 4% of research institutions nationwide, and reflect the University’s momentum in becoming a hub for world-class graduate education.
